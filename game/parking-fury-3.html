VEX X3M

★★★★★ 26 votes

Stick figures are often perplexed by all the scenarios they find themselves in, and the Vex team is no different when being in Vex X3M. It seems that this group of stick figures wants to be the most extreme, and the way they're going about it is to get themselves some motorcycles and drive them along the most twisted paths.

About Vex X3M

The thing about motorcycle games is that they're enjoyable as both skill challenges and just sending motorcycles crashing all over the place. This game may have you trying to avoid crashing, but it's no big deal if it happens. The stick figures are prepared to crash everywhere and won't let some wild tracks dissuade them.

How to Play

Vex X3M is a 2D arcade game about a stick figure riding a motorcycle along tracks using the keyboard.

Controls

  • W/Up Arrow - Accelerate
  • S/Down Arrow - Decelerate/Reverse
  • A/Left Arrow - Tilt Left
  • D/Right Arrow - Tilt Right
