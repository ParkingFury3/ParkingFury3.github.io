Mega Ramp: Bike Racing Tracks is an adrenaline-fueled adventure that puts your biking skills to the ultimate test. Race across towering ramps, perform gravity-defying stunts, and conquer impossible tracks in this thrilling bike racing game.

About Mega Ramp: Bike Racing Tracks

This game is a high-stakes challenge designed for thrill-seekers. Ride powerful bikes across sky-high tracks filled with ramps, loops, and other obstacles. It's all about balance, speed, and daring tricks as you race to the finish line.

How to Play

Mega Ramp: Bike Racing Tracks is a 3D stunt racing game where players control a bike to navigate extreme tracks. Perform stunts, maintain control, and reach the finish line to win!

Controls

  • W/Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S/Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • A/Left Arrow: Tilt Left
  • D/Right Arrow: Tilt Right
  • Space: Perform Stunt
