Test your parking and driving skills in the immersive world of Parking Fury 3D! Navigate realistic environments, handle various vehicles, and tackle increasingly complex parking challenges. Precision and control are the keys to mastering this game.

About Parking Fury 3D

Parking Fury 3D takes the popular parking series to a new level with stunning 3D graphics and dynamic gameplay. From tight city streets to spacious parking lots, the game challenges players to maneuver vehicles perfectly without hitting obstacles. The open-world design adds an exciting twist, letting you explore and find the best routes to your destination.

How to Play

Drive carefully through the city and park your vehicle in the designated spots. Avoid collisions with other cars, barriers, and pedestrians. Complete tasks quickly and accurately to earn higher scores!

Controls

  • W/Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S/Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • A/Left Arrow: Steer Left
  • D/Right Arrow: Steer Right
  • Shift: Activate Nitro
  • Spacebar: Handbrake
